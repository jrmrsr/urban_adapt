Who are we?

We are a group of engineering alumni from the University of Waterloo who want to make a difference. Our journey started when we won the Rain It In competition and decided to take an idea and turn it into a tool for the public. Our goal is to educate people on stormwater and how to manage it.

Urban Adapt is a collection of practical summaries, guidelines and opportunities to reduce the cost of implementing stormwater management. It was created to empower property owners to create resilient and sustainable communities.

Coming Soon

Credit Program Application Web-App

We are working on creating a web application that matches property owners with flooding protection measures that make sense for them. By using an algorithm that considers each individual property, the application helps property owners take action and protect themselves from flooding damage. Once a user has successfully implemented these measures, the application allows the user to apply for regional financial incentives quickly and easily.

If your organization or municipality is interested in helping develop this application, please contact us at urbanadapt@urbanadapt.ca.
